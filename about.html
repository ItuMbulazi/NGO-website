Who are we

You-Nique is an organisation based in kimberley that advocates socioeconomic issues through various mediums of art.it was co-founded -and is run by Genevieve "GennArt" Clarke;an Art practitioner (University pf the Free State Fine Arts graduate) as well as Founder and managing director of GennArt and Macduff Valashiya;a performing poet and founder of a kimberley-based poetry group, Cullinan Creatives,a performing poet and certified digital marketer.it was founded in 2021,amidst the scourge that is gender based violence and initiated an online campaign to raise awareness around GBV.

OUR VISION

To become the parargon of activism throught art and curate instruction as to how creative outlet can be a form of therapy and remedy for various psychological and socioeconomic issues. To establish a culture of colloboration between youth organisations and commercial enterprises of similar googleapisand visions

OUR MISSION

To advocate for art as a form of therapy and remedy for psychological,socials and economic issues. To identify and advocate against socioeconomic through the facilitation of skills development workshops. To colloborate with organisations on projects that aim to raise awareness and address issues as effectively as possible. To promote art organisations and practitioners through colloboration and highlighting of respective skills sets in the creative arts.
